A Visual Designer / SweepBank

SweepBank is a smart financial app that empowers shopping and banking experience through financial insights, tailored offers and payment options. One of the key features of the app is Sweep Deals, a digital shopping platform that offers customers a shopping place with discounts and loyalty points on purchases within the app.

In collaboration with the marketing team, we created a new SweepBank website. Working closely with developers and product designers we created a new web design system. We put a strong focus on clarity by following a minimalist design approach. The common visual aspects of SweepBank image language is: motion, bright colors, bold stories, excitement/thrill and young. I re-used the website visual language for various marketing assets such as emails, animations and banners.
