A Visual Designer at SweepBank

SweepBank is a smart financial app that empowers shopping and banking experience through financial insights, tailored offers and payment options. One of the key features of the app is Sweep Deals, a digital shopping platform that offers customers a shopping place with discounts and loyalty points on purchases within the app.

I worked in a marketing team and together we created a brand-new SweepBank website. Working closely with developers we built the design system for web. The challenge was to create a simple marketing website scalable over time using global components, accessibile from mobile and desktop. We put a strong focus on clarity by following a minimalist design approach. Motion, bright colors, bold stories, excitement/thrill and young were the common visual aspects of SweepBank image language to create a new experience. I re-used the website visual language for various marketing assets such as emails, animations and banners.
