SweepBank

SweepBank is a smart financial app that empowers shopping and banking experience through financial insights, tailored offers and payment options. One of the key features of the app is Sweep Deals, a digital shopping platform that offers customers a shopping place with discounts and loyalty points on purchases within the app.

In collaboration with the marketing team, we created a new SweepBank website. As a visual designer, I was responsible for creating a good web design system, working closely with developers and product designers. The existing brand book helped guide my design decisions and the look and feel of the website. We put a strong focus on clarity by following a minimalist design approach. I used our typeface as the main design element to create fun and bold visuals. Sweep text filled with different images, gradients, outlined text or a repeating text patterns are also very characteristic of the brand. Shapes are also an important aspect of design expressing moods and leading the eye. I re-used the website visual language for various marketing assets such as emails, animations, social media and banners.
