SweepBank

During my tenure at SweepBank, I was an essential member of the Marketing team. Together with members from other teams ranging from Business to Developers, we created the SweepBank website for Global and local markets including Finland, Latvia, Germany, and Denmark.

What they do

SweepBank is a smart financial app that empowers shopping and banking experience through financial insights, tailored offers and payment options. One of the key features of the app is Sweep Deals, a digital shopping platform that offers customers a shopping place with discounts and loyalty points on purchases within the app.

The situation

We answered the challenge of creating a visually compelling and scalable website that represented the SweepBank brand. Using global components applied to country-specific markets to compel our prospective customers to engage, learn, and join SweepBank. Applying a strong emphasis on the mobile experience, we applied a minimalist design approach with round shapes, bold, bright colours, high contrast type, all combined with nature-based photography to bring a sense of calm and an uplifting atmosphere. Due to the success of the design language I created, we were able to apply what was visually developed for the website across various marketing assets and other channels from Email, Social Media, and Affiliate asset packages.
